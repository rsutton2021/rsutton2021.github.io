Hello, my name is Rob

Welcome to my portfolio

Former newspaper sub-editor, page designer and news editor turned trainee developer. Based in northern England, I’m currently learning full stack development with IT Career Switch and Codecademy.
I am working to apply my attention to detail, layout and typography skills to a new digital career which draws upon previous HTML and scripting experience.
Away from my desk I like walking in the Lake District and landscape photography.
